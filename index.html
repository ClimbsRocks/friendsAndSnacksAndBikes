A quick intro to who we are and what we do

Here's some info about the rides:

We meet each Thursday at 7AM for coffee & snacks at various coffee houses around the city, then go off and do a ride that gets most people back and into work between 9 and 10. We generally send out the coffee place the Wednesday before the ride, but you can plan on it being at 7AM every Thursday.

Every other week we do the Marin Headlands (Hawk Hill- a pretty substantial hill if you're just getting into biking for longer than 2 miles at a time, or a really fun one if clip-ins are your thing). Then on the other weeks, we do a more relaxed city ride up Twin Peaks or some other small hill in the city.

All rides are no-drop to the top (there's always going to be someone to ride with you- no person left behind!), but once at the top, some people have to splinter off rapidly to go to work, while others might extend the ride a bit longer, or head home at a relaxed pace.

This is definitely a social ride, not a competitive ride, but if you're the competitive type, there are normally a handful of people around who prefer their coffee with a substantial helping of sweat :)

Overall, it's a fun and joking group, and one of the best ways to wake up that I've found yet!

Hope to have you join us one of these weeks!
